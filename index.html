Trusted & Experienced

You can completely trust us. All cleaners work on the staff of the company, are thoroughly tested and trained. In case of damage to the client’s property due to the fault of the cliner, we will refund you the full cost.

Individual Approach

During the first cleaning all your wishes are recorded by the client in the client’s card. The next time you do not have to repeat the instructions. Moreover, if you tell the clinics about the frequency of certain jobs, for example, washing windows, the clinic will do it by default on an established schedule.

Respect & Goodwill

We respect our customers, their families and pets, and we try to make our stay in your home as comfortable as possible for you. We use only hypoallergenic cleaning products of the highest quality.

Professional supplies

We use only professional cleaning products - effective and eco-friendly. The products are hypoallergenic and do not harm either people or animals.
